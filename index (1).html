About Veritax

Truth in every tax credit — India's first AI-powered GST ITC denial defence platform

The Problem Veritax Solves

Thousands of Indian businesses lose legitimate GST Input Tax Credit every year — not because they did anything wrong, but because suppliers failed to file returns or because a statutory deadline was missed. Two provisions are responsible for virtually all ITC denials: Section 16(4) time-bar and Rule 36(4) GSTR-2A/2B mismatch. Veritax catches the risk before it happens, and builds the legal case when it does — classifying every notice as Category 1 (valid), Category 2 (contestable), Category 3 (likely illegal), or Procedural Defect.

Why Veritax Exists

Veritax was built from firsthand experience of how GST ITC denial is breaking Indian businesses — especially MSMEs who cannot afford the legal fees to fight every notice. The system is designed to level the playing field: give every CA firm and every business the same legal intelligence that was previously available only to large corporates with full in-house tax teams.
