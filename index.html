About Narentis

Truth in every tax credit — India's first AI-powered GST ITC denial defence platform

The Problem Narentis Solves

Thousands of Indian businesses lose legitimate GST Input Tax Credit every year — not because they did anything wrong, but because suppliers failed to file returns or because a statutory deadline was missed. Narentis catches the risk before it happens, and builds the legal case when it does — classifying every notice as Category 1 (valid), Category 2 (contestable), Category 3 (likely illegal), or Procedural Defect.
