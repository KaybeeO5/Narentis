# Veritax

**AI-powered GST ITC Denial Pattern Recognition & Early Warning System**

> "Truth in every tax credit."

Veritax analyses GST Input Tax Credit denial notices against 75 verified court judgments, classifies the legal risk, and recommends the right course of action — in under 60 seconds.

## What Veritax Does

- **Notice Analyser** — Paste any GST DRC-01 or adjudication order. Veritax runs dual-track legal analysis (Section 16(4) and Rule 36(4)), assigns a Legal Sustainability Score (1–10), cites the most relevant verified court judgment, and produces a structured Legal Risk Report.

- **Supplier Risk Monitor** — Scores every supplier GREEN / AMBER / RED / BLACK based on GSTR-1 filing compliance history. Provides 30-day advance warning before a mismatch hits your GSTR-2B — the only window before GSTR-3B hard-locking closes the door.

- **Case Law Library** — 75 verified High Court and Supreme Court judgments on GST ITC denial, searchable and filterable by provision, outcome, court, and category. Zero hallucinated citations.

## Legal Provisions Covered

- Section 16(4) CGST Act — Time bar denial
- Section 16(2)(c) — Supplier non-payment of tax
- Section 16(2)(aa) — GSTR-2B linkage condition
- Rule 36(4) CGST Rules — GSTR-2A/2B mismatch restriction
- Section 16(5) — Finance Act 2024 retrospective relief
- Section 73 — SCN validity and procedural requirements
- CBIC Circular 183/15/2022 — Mandatory mismatch procedure

## Validation

- **10/10** accuracy on synthetic test notices across all denial branches
- **Zero** hallucinated case citations
- Tested across Track A (Section 16(4)), Track B (Rule 36(4)), Dual Track, and Procedural Defect branches
